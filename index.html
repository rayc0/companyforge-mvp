Approval system

Four bands. Full control.

Set the risk tolerance for each venture. The AI respects it absolutely.

Band A
Fully autonomous

AI runs the task end-to-end with no human review. Ideal for research, drafting, and summarisation.

Band B
Auto with log

Runs automatically but creates a detailed audit entry. You review after the fact.

Band C
Pause for approval

AI drafts and pauses. You review, edit, then approve. Nothing external happens without your sign-off.

Band D
Hard blocked

Client-side block. AI cannot execute contracts, payments, or high-risk actions — even if instructed.

The Payment Layer

Every agent payment is a post-quantum signed mandate.

When CompanyForge executes a payment, it calls PQSafe — the cryptographic trust primitive for the agent economy. Every disbursement is a SpendEnvelope: post-quantum signed, revocable mid-flight, logged with a hash that survives regulatory audit. Band D is hard-blocked client-side. The agent cannot wire money without your explicit confirmation.
